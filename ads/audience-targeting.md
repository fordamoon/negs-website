## LOOKALIKE AUDIENCE STRATEGY

**Phase 1 (Day 1–14):** Run interest-based cold audiences only. Collect pixel data and lead form completions.

**Phase 2 (Day 15–30):** Build a 1% Lookalike audience based on:
- AI Readiness Score completers (primary seed)
- Website visitors who spent 30+ seconds on site (secondary seed)

**Phase 3 (Day 30+):** Layer a 1% Lookalike based on intro call bookings once you have 20+ conversions. This becomes your highest-value audience.

**Note:** Lookalikes in small geo areas (New England only) will be limited in size. That's fine — you want precise, not massive.

---

## RETARGETING AUDIENCES

**Retarget Pool 1 — Website visitors**
- All visitors, last 30 days
- Exclude anyone who already completed the AI Readiness Score

**Retarget Pool 2 — Engaged but didn't convert**
- Visited site 2+ times in 14 days
- Spent 60+ seconds on site
- Did NOT complete AI Readiness Score or book a call

**Retarget Pool 3 — Partial completions**
- Started AI Readiness Score but didn't finish
- Visited pricing or services page but didn't take action

## BUDGET SPLIT — $80 CAMPAIGN

### Week 1–2: Testing Phase ($80 total)

| Allocation | Amount | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Cold Audience A (Tourism/Hospitality) | $25 | Highest-intent vertical |
| Cold Audience B (Marina/Outdoor) | $20 | Secondary vertical test |
| Cold Audience C (Broad SMB) | $15 | Broad signal collection |
| Retargeting (all pools) | $20 | Convert warm traffic |

### Daily spend: ~$5.70/day over 14 days

### Key rules:
1. **Run all three cold audiences as separate ad sets.** Do not combine — you need to see which vertical responds.
2. **Kill any ad set that spends $15 with zero clicks.** Reallocate to the winner.
3. **Retargeting won't fire until you have pixel traffic.** Start retarget ads on Day 3–4 once the cold campaigns have driven initial visits.
4. **Use the AI Readiness Score as the conversion event.** Not "link click" — actual completions. Optimize for conversions from Day 1.

---

## AD FORMAT RECOMMENDATIONS

**Cold ads:** Single image or short video (under 30 sec). Image of Matthew or a clean branded graphic — not stock photography. Authenticity wins in this market.

**Retargeting ads:** Carousel showing the three service tiers OR a single testimonial-style image with a direct quote.

**Placement:** Feed + Stories on both Facebook and Instagram. Disable Audience Network — burns budget on low-quality placements at this spend level.

## SCALING NOTES

At $80, this is a signal-finding campaign, not a scale campaign. The goal is to answer three questions:

1. **Which vertical responds?** Tourism, marina/outdoor, or broad SMB?
2. **Which tagline hooks?** Test 2–3 hooks per audience and let CTR decide.
3. **Does the AI Readiness Score convert cold traffic?** If yes, this funnel scales. If no, the landing page needs work before spending more.

Once you have answers: double down on the winning audience, kill the losers, and scale to $200–400/month with confidence.
