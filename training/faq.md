### 1. What does New England Growth Studio actually do?

We help tourism and hospitality businesses in New England figure out where their marketing money is working and where it's not. Then we help them fix it. We do this through a combination of a software tool called Headwaters and hands-on consulting — from a two-week sprint all the way up to an ongoing growth partnership.

### 2. What is Headwaters?

Headwaters is a marketing attribution tool. You upload your marketing spend by channel and your booking numbers, and it tells you which channels are actually driving revenue. It uses the same math that big companies have used for decades — we just built a version that works with a spreadsheet and costs $297 a year instead of $50K.

### 3. How is this different from what Google Analytics or Facebook already tells me?

Every ad platform takes credit for everything. Facebook says it drove the booking. Google says the same one. TripAdvisor agrees. Headwaters is independent — it uses your actual spend and your actual bookings to figure out the truth, not what any platform claims.

### 4. How much does Headwaters cost?

$297/year. One price. Full access. Unlimited uploads. 30-day money-back guarantee.

### 5. What if it doesn't work for my business?

If you don't get something actionable in the first 30 days, full refund. No questions. No hoops.

### 6. What kind of data do I need?

Your marketing spend by channel (Google, Facebook, TripAdvisor, print — whatever you use) and your booking numbers. Both broken down by month or by week. Six months minimum. Twelve is better. Most businesses already have this — it's just scattered across different platforms.

### 7. I don't have a spreadsheet with all this. Can you help me pull it together?

Yes. That's one of the things we do in the AI + Growth Readiness Sprint. We help you gather your data, clean it up, and run it. If you just need a hand formatting the CSV, reach out and we'll walk you through it — no charge.

### 8. What's the AI Readiness Score?

A free, 10-question online assessment that tells you how ready your business is to use AI and data to grow. Takes two minutes. You get a score, a tier, and a specific next step. No payment required. No sales call required to see your results.

### 9. What are the consulting services?

Three tiers. The AI + Growth Readiness Sprint is a two-week deep dive — we assess your data, systems, and marketing, and hand you a 90-day roadmap. The Funnel & Follow-Up Automation Build is a 4–6 week engagement where we actually build out your lead capture, CRM, and conversion flows. The Fractional Growth Architect is a monthly retainer where I act as your senior growth partner on an ongoing basis.

### 10. How much does the consulting cost?

Pricing depends on scope. The Sprint is the entry point and the most affordable. Reach out and I'll give you an honest number based on your situation. I'm not going to waste your time or mine quoting something that doesn't fit.

### 11. I only use one marketing channel. Is Headwaters still useful?

Probably not, and I'll tell you that. Headwaters is designed for businesses spending across two or more channels. If you're only on Google, you don't need an attribution tool — you need to make sure your Google setup is dialed in. I can help with that too, but Headwaters isn't the right tool for a single-channel business.

### 12. I'm not a tech person. Can I actually use this?

Yes. If you can open a spreadsheet and click a button, you can use Headwaters. The output is a chart and plain-language percentages — not a technical report. I built it specifically for operators who don't have a data team and don't want one.

### 13. What industries do you work with?

Tourism operators, hospitality businesses (inns, B&Bs, boutique hotels), marinas and boat rentals, campgrounds, guided tour and charter companies, ski areas, and outdoor recreation brands. All in New England. That's the focus.

### 14. Why only New England?

Because I live here, I know the market, and these businesses are underserved. The big marketing analytics companies don't build for 12-room inns and seasonal boat rentals. I do. And being local means I can walk in your door — which matters in this industry.

16+ years in digital media, growth strategy, and omnichannel marketing. I was one of the first 300 employees at Pandora. I worked at Apple. I've built and sold at the highest levels. Now I'm applying that same thinking to small businesses that deserve the same quality of growth infrastructure.

That's not what I do. I'm not an agency. I don't run your ads or manage your social media. I build the systems that tell you where to spend and help you automate the parts that shouldn't require a person. If you need someone to manage your day-to-day marketing, I can recommend people. But my value is in the strategy and the infrastructure.

### 17. How long before I see results?

With Headwaters, you'll have an actionable report in 60 seconds. With the Sprint, you'll have a complete 90-day roadmap in two weeks. How fast the results show up after that depends on how quickly you act on what we find. But most businesses I work with identify at least one immediate cost savings in the first session.

### 18. Is my data safe?

Because I'll tell you when something isn't right for your business. I've turned away clients who weren't ready. I've refunded people who didn't get value.
